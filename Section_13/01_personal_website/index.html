About me

I am a brazilian autistic individual, born in 1983, when that kind of diagnosis was not something that existed in the way we know about it today. Because of the lack of a proper diagnosis, I wasn't aware about the fact I'm autistic until a few years ago, when my nephew was disgnosed with autism and so was I, by his doctors. The treatment for autism here in Brazil is not something affordable and so, being aknowledge about the fact I'm autsitic just helped me to understand why I have some specific comportamental behavior traces that make me be who I am.

My hyperfocus is centered into technology and science, and I always work hard to always give into that as much as I can. Because if this, my first contact with a computer was when I was 10 years old, in a school specialized into dealing with gifted individuals (yes, that time, that was the diagnosis I received), where I wrote my first line of code, using Cobol to make a Pac-Man game which run from a 5.2" floppy disk in a old IBM computer.

My Bsc. degree was attained on 2004, when I was 20 years old, on Computational Physics. My main area of work, back in the day, was physical phenomena simulation, with focus into Quantic Chaos, while studing the behavior of sub-atomic particles when those are confined within parabolic magnetic fields, using Maple (a programming language used by MapleSoft™ ), C and OpenGL, by creating applications to simulate their movement in a 3d field space. I also have used, when necessary, other programming languages such as Pascal, Java and Fortran for smaller projects when I was still a member of the university students research team.

I have also worked with geophysics, while creating data processing routines to optmize the already existing routines used generally by most of the Brazilian prospecting companies, while making use of the languages GCX and GS (used by Oasis Montaj, an applcation developed by Seequent - formely known as Geosoft ).

In 2010 I started streaming on Twitch, and that pushed me to a different road. That was where while trying to make something that would fit my standards, I learned some new skills to be used into my streams. It was when I started using Python 2.7.13 to create add-ons to a chatbot application I use on my streams (Streamlab Chatbot) as well I decided taking some courses to work with graphical design.

From that day, I changed my main field of work from Physics to the graphical design market while also self-learning web development, by reading official documentaion over technologies used for web.

In 2023 I decided to start taking online courses so I could attain more knowledge about web development and update / solidify the previous knowledge I had attained by self-learning, while creating a webdev portifolio and obtaning certificates.

I also give private classes (over webdev) and have recently starting to work into content creation so I can provide courses into platforms such as Udemy, focusing on my main dev stack (Noje.js, Postgres / Supabase, Material Ui, TailwindCss, React/Next.js and Electron.js).

When not working, I take my free time to play some games, to stream (even though I have not streamed in a whike do the amount of work I'm doing recently) or to keep studying and learning more about the things that are related to my hyperfocus.
